Pricing & Performance Space Requirements

Base Price: $200 per Performer per 20 Minutes + $50 for safety person (unless hiring as a second performer).
Use the Contact page to get a quote for your event.

Performance Space Requirements

Minimum space for 1 performer 15ft. Square
Minimum space for 2 performers (same time) 30ft. Square
Minimum space for 3 performers (same time) 40ft. Square
Minimum safe distance to audience 10ft. Circle
Minimum Ceiling Height 12ft. High
Minimum Ceiling Height for Firebreathing Outside only
